NutriDel

Healthy and accessible foods straight to your door!

Our mission is to make sure low-income communities get the healthy goods and produce they need at a low and reasonable price. From every order we receive, we use some of our profits to improve our business, expand our services, hire and create new jobs for the community, etc. We can A) provide healthy, organic and natural produce to low -income families at an inexpensive price and B) help our communities by creating jobs and helping them save money with these inexpensive prices.

Here at NutriDel, our goal to improve the nutritional conditions in food deserts across NYC through delivery of fresh produce sourced from farmers who engage in sustainable farming practices. From every order we recieve, we use some of our profits to improve our business, create new jobs and hire more employees.

We provide a service that is cheaper, healthier, and more effective than shopping at the supermarket, saving you time and money. We double-check with the farmers that we buy from to ensure that the produce being provided is of the highest quality and is also produced in an eco-friendly way.

Once you order something off the app, we receive what exactly you ordered. We contact the closest shipping location and try to get your goods to you as fast as possible. Depending on your marked location, the one responsible for your delivery will have it delivered right to your door. Try to contact us back and make sure you're super specific on what is wrong with your order. If you would like an exchange, we can exchange the item that is wrong with your order. Once you get your order, make sure you're able rate us depending on your experience. Our deliveries are set to your location, access your location by typing where exactly you're located so we can be able to delivery it to you as soon as possible.
